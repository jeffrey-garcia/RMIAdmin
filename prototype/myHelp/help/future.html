Future
RMIAdmin is developed with a great vision of managing any computer on anywhere and at any time.

It was originally created for the purpose of automating server administration tasks, as a result to increase efficiency and effectiveness, soon we discovered that the software itself could in fact do a lot more than just server task automation.

Rather than simply managing remote servers via local client, we also provide customization and extensibility, such as "Command Line Option", which allows administrator/developer to further automate tasks via scripting or batch job.

We also have a set of JAVATM API opened for programmers to interface the RMIAdmin with other software, such that more creative ideas could be implemented and therefore give birth to other inspirating software.

Indeed the blueprint of next version RMIAdmin has already been justified, our intension is to implement RMIAdmin on most computer platforms, as well as mobile devices and embedded systems, moreover the integration of more innovative ideas and inspirating technology into RMIAdmin will be our major task and direction to future.
